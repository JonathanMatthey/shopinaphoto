Cheryl Cole Flying Out To America To Be With Ashley Cole?

Could that be the reason why she was at the US Enbassy?...

  • Share 
Photo: Splash News

The world went into a bit of a frenzy on Wednesday when word got round that Cheryl Cole had been spotted at the US Embassy, because it was that the first time that the star had been seen since getting sacked from the X Factor USA.

Having thought that the star would never return to America following her unsuccessful attempt to crack the country, it looks as though the star will jet out to the US once again to meet up with friend and Black Eyed Peas frontman, Will.i.am AND ex husband Ashley Cole...

According to the Sun, Ashley, who is currently in the States has reportedly extended his holiday so that he will be there when Cheryl jets in early next week.

A source revealed: "Ashley was due back yesterday on her birthday but altered his plans. It's unlikely he would have showed up at her party anyway but it would still have been a gesture to be back in time for her birthday.

"He could only be staying put for one reason - Cheryl."

Will the US be the place where Cheryl and Ashley fall in love again?
